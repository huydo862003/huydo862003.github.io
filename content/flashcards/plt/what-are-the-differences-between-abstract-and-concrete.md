- Concrete/Surface syntax: The language that the programmers actually programs in - usually a sequence of characters.
- Structural/Abstract syntax: The more structured language, easier to reason about & worked on by compiler implementations. Often represented by [Abstract syntax tree (AST)](/journeys/plt/concepts/abstract-syntax-tree-ast).
