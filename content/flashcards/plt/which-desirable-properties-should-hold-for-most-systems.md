Should hold:

- Desired property: Determinacy of one-step evaluation relation.
- Desired property: Uniqueness of normal forms.
- Desired property: Value is in normal form.

Might not hold:

- Desired property: Normal form implies values.
- Desired property: Termination of evaluation.
